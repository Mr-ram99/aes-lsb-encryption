How it works?

  1. Encryption
    1. Click on encrypt button.
    2. Enter your text and upload an image/audio.
    3. You will see an encrypted image/audio on screen, click on image/audio to download it.

  2. Decryption
    1. Click on decrypt button.
    2. upload an image/audio file.
    3. You will see decrypted text on screen.
